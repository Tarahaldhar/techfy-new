Meet Our Founder — Tara

With over 5–6 years of experience in the IT industry, Mai Tara's journey has been one of constant learning, bold decisions, and unshakable determination. But alongside her job, her entrepreneurial spirit never rested. She spent countless hours freelancing, learning how to manage projects independently, building client relationships, and sharpening her skills beyond the 9 to 5 routine.
